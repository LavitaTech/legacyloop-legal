Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LegacyLoop, provided by LavitaTech (“LegacyLoop,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, stores, shares, protects, and deletes information when you use the LegacyLoop mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

LegacyLoop is designed to help users organize important information and configure trusted access preferences. LegacyLoop does not provide legal, financial, tax, medical, estate planning, or professional advice and does not replace legally executed documents, estate plans, wills, trusts, powers of attorney, advance directives, beneficiary designations, or advice from qualified professionals.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account information

  • Account identifiers, including user ID and Legacy ID.
  • Email address and authentication-related account data.
  • Display name or profile details you choose to provide.
  • Account status, account settings, and app preferences.

1.2 Vault content you choose to store

Depending on the features you use, LegacyLoop may allow you to store, organize, or manage sensitive personal content, including:

  • Documents, files, images, videos, notes, and instructions.
  • Emergency information and care-related instructions.
  • Account guidance, password hints, asset notes, or account notes.
  • Messages intended for trusted contacts or beneficiaries.
  • Trusted contact, beneficiary, emergency contact, and verification contact details.
  • Release preferences, inactivity settings, access permissions, and audit-related records.

1.3 Contact and invite information

  • Names, emails, relationship labels, and invite statuses for trusted people you add.
  • Access roles and permissions you assign to trusted contacts or beneficiaries.
  • Invite links, invite responses, and related workflow information.

1.4 Information collected automatically

  • Device and app information, such as device type, operating system, app version, and language.
  • Authentication, security, and activity signals needed to operate the Service.
  • Diagnostics, crash logs, performance data, and error records used to maintain reliability.
  • Notification tokens used to deliver reminders, alerts, or account-related notifications.

1.5 Support information

If you contact support, we may collect your Legacy ID, account email, device information, issue description, screenshots, and correspondence needed to investigate and respond.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, secure, and improve LegacyLoop.
  • Authenticate users and protect accounts.
  • Store and organize user vault content based on user actions.
  • Enable trusted contact, beneficiary, emergency access, and release preference workflows.
  • Deliver notifications, reminders, warnings, and account-related messages.
  • Process account deletion, content deletion, and privacy-related requests.
  • Provide customer support and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • Detect, prevent, investigate, and respond to abuse, fraud, security risks, or policy violations.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect rights, safety, and property.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

3.1 With people you choose

LegacyLoop is designed around user-controlled sharing. Information may be made available to trusted contacts, emergency contacts, beneficiaries, or verification contacts only according to the access settings, permissions, release rules, and workflows configured in the app.

3.2 Service providers

We may use third-party service providers to operate the Service, including hosting, authentication, databases, storage, push notifications, crash reporting, analytics, security, and customer support tools. These providers may process information on our behalf only as needed to provide services to us.

3.3 Legal, safety, and enforcement

We may disclose information when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, enforce our terms or policies, investigate abuse or security issues, protect users, or protect rights, safety, and property.

3.4 Business transfers

If LegacyLoop or LavitaTech is involved in a merger, acquisition, reorganization, financing, sale of assets, or similar transaction, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality and legal protections.

3.5 No data resale

LegacyLoop is not designed to resell user vault data, personal documents, account guidance, messages, trusted contact records, or beneficiary records.

4. User Control and Account Deletion

You control the information you choose to add to LegacyLoop. Where supported by the app, you may edit or delete certain content, update trusted contacts, change access permissions, and request or initiate account deletion.

When you delete your account through the app, LegacyLoop is designed to remove your account and associated user-controlled content from active systems, including user documents and related records, subject to technical, legal, security, backup, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, and compliance limitations.

Some information may be retained for a limited period where reasonably necessary, such as logs, deletion records, support communications, abuse prevention records, security records, or records required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably needed to provide the Service, fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with law, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, maintain security, prevent fraud, and support account deletion or restoration processes.

6. Security

7. Sensitive Information

LegacyLoop may allow users to store sensitive personal, family, financial, logistical, account-related, or end-of-life information. You are responsible for choosing what to store, ensuring you have the right to upload it, and assigning trusted access carefully.

8. Trusted Contacts and Beneficiaries

If you add another person as a trusted contact, beneficiary, emergency contact, or verification contact, you are responsible for providing accurate information and confirming that your use of their information complies with applicable law and any obligations you may have to that person.

9. Third-Party Services

The Service may use or link to third-party services. Third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies and terms. We are not responsible for third-party privacy, security, or data practices.

10. Children

LegacyLoop is not intended for children under 13 or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact support.

11. International Use

If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other locations where we or our service providers operate.

13. Contact
