What to Include

To help us investigate quickly, include the following details:

Legacy ID: Account Email: Device Model: iOS Version: App Version: Issue: Steps to Reproduce: Screenshots Attached: Yes/No

Account Deletion and Data Requests

If you want to delete your account, use the in-app Account Settings deletion option when available. If you are unable to complete deletion in the app, contact support and include your Legacy ID and account email.

Account deletion is intended to remove your account and associated user-controlled content from active systems, subject to technical, legal, security, fraud-prevention, backup, and compliance limitations.

Important Limitations

LegacyLoop helps users organize important information and trusted access settings. It does not provide legal, financial, tax, medical, estate planning, or professional advice and does not replace legally executed documents or advice from qualified professionals.
